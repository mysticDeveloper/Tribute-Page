Kevin Smith

Director of Clerks and the creator of the Smodcast Network

Major events in Smith's life


  • 1970: On August 2 in Red Bank, New Jersey Kevin Patrick Smith was born

  • 1991: Smith decides to go to film school after seeing the movie Slacker

  • 1994: Smith's first film Clerks was screened at the Sundance Film Festival.

  • 1994: Clerks was bought by Miramax.

  • 1994: Clerks won both the Prix de la Jeunesse and the International Critics' Week Prize

  • 1997: Chasing Amy is released. This was considered Smith's best film

  • 1999: On April 25 Kevin Smith married Jennifer Schwalbach

  • 1999: Dogma is screened at Cannes Film Festival and recieves three nominations

  • 1999: On June 26 Kevin Smith's daughter Harley Quinn Smith was born

  • 2001: Jay and Silent Bob Strike Back is released

  • 2006: Kevin Smith released Clerks 2

  • 2006: Clerks 2 won the Audience Award at the Edinburgh Film Festival

  • 2006: Clerks 2 won the Orbit Dirtiest Mouth Award at the MTV Movie Awards

  • 2006: Clerks 2 received an eight-minute standing ovation at Cannes International Film Festival

  • 2007: Kevin Smith Launched the Smodcast Network

  • 2008: On January 18 Kevin Smith began shooting Zack and Miri Make a Porno

  • 2008: On October 31 Zack and Miri Make a Porno was released

  • 2009: Smith directs Copout and after working with Bruce Willis decides to only do independant film

  • 2011: Smith's film Red State is released

  • 2011: Red State won Best Motion Picture award at the 2011 Sitges Film Festival

  • 2013: Kevin Smith begins his True North movie trilogy by filming Tusk

  • 2014: Tusk is released

  • 2014: Kevin Smith begins filming Yoga Hosers staring his daughter Harley Quinn Smith

  • 2016: Yoga Hosers is released
